Questions

General

  • What is a maker?
  • What role does the maker movement play in health?
  • What if you aren't a maker yourself? How does this connect to being a medical librarian?
  • What are the differences between consumer technology and what faculty/researchers use?

Space

  • How do you structure your services?
  • Where did you get funding?
  • What resources are being most utilized in your library and what are they being used for?
  • How are you managing copyright and intellectual property concerns?

Trends

  • What do you feel are the trends in 3D printing and use of other maker technologies? Which are happening on your campus?
  • What are unexpected costs?
  • What are different ways 3D printing is being used for health?
  • How can medical librarians partner with other campus or community makerspaces to support relevant activities?
